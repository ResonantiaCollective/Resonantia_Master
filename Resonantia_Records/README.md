**Resonantia Records** is the audio production, release, and broadcast pillar of the Resonantia Collective.

It exists to translate the Codex into **real-world signal**:  
music that is released, performed, archived, and preserved with intent.

---

## ROLE WITHIN RESONANTIA

Resonantia Records operates as:

- A **music label**
- A **performance archive**
- A **broadcast layer** for canon transmissions
- A **professional release and asset system**

Not all audio produced here is canon.  
All canon audio is archived here.

---

## 00_ADMIN  
**Administrative & Operational Authority**

Contains non-creative documentation, including:

- Contracts and agreements  
- Licensing and rights management  
- Planning and release strategy  
- Financial records  
- Internal operational documents  

This folder is strictly separated from creative assets.

---

## 01_ARTISTS  
**Artist & Operative Profiles**

Per-artist directories.

Each artist folder may contain:

- Biography and identity notes  
- Press kits  
- Branding assets  
- Track and set notes  
- Planning and strategy  
- Associated sets and performance files  

This structure scales as new operatives are onboarded.

---

## 02_RELEASES  
**Official Resonantia Records Catalog**

Houses all formal label releases, indexed by catalog number.

Each release folder includes:

- Mixdowns and masters  
- Stems (when applicable)  
- Artwork and cover variants  
- Release notes and documentation  
- Metadata and distribution assets  
- ISRC and rights information  

**Naming convention:**  
`RR###_Artist_Title`

This folder represents the **canonical discography** of Resonantia Records.

---

## 03_SETS  
**DJ Sets & Live Transmissions**

Non-catalog performances and continuous mixes.

Organized by artist and session, including:

- Live performances  
- Studio sessions  
- Promotional or experimental mixes  

Certain sets may be designated as **Canon Transmissions**  
(see Living Bible for canonical designation).

---

## 04_SAMPLE_PACKS  
**Sound Design & Tooling**

Sample packs intended for production, collaboration, or release.

Includes:

- Raw exports  
- Processed sounds  
- Final pack versions  
- Pack documentation and metadata  

May include both free and commercial assets.

---

## 05_ASSET_LIBRARY  
**Shared Visual & Brand Resources**

Centralized design assets used across Resonantia Records.

Includes:

- Logo and sigil variants  
- Typography kits  
- Social and promotional templates  
- Key visual resources  

This folder ensures visual consistency across releases and broadcasts.

---

## FINAL NOTE

This structure mirrors professional label operations while remaining  
fully integrated into the broader Resonantia Universe.

It is designed to:

- Scale with additional artists  
- Preserve long-term archives  
- Support public release and private operations  
- Maintain clarity between canon, release, and experimentation  

Resonantia Records is not a side branch.  
It is the **voice** of the system.
