Resonantia Records is the audio production, release, and branding pillar of the Resonantia Universe.

---

## 00_ADMIN
Operational and administrative files:
- Contracts
- Licensing
- Planning
- Financials
- Strategy documents

---

## 01_ARTISTS
Per-artist folders.

Each contains:
- Bio
- Press kit
- Branding assets
- Track notes
- Plans & strategy
- Sets and performance files

---

## 02_RELEASES
The Resonantia Records catalog (RR###).

Each release folder includes:
- Mixdowns
- Masters
- Stems
- Artwork
- Release notes
- Metadata
- ISRC information

Naming format:  
**RR###_Artist_Title**

---

## 03_SETS
DJ sets and live sessions.

Organized by artist:
- Live sets
- Studio sets
- Promo mixes

---

## 04_SAMPLE_PACKS
Sound design packs.

Includes:
- Raw exports
- Processed sounds
- Final pack versions
- Pack metadata

---

## 05_ASSET_LIBRARY
Shared templates and design resources:
- Logo variants
- Typography kits
- Social templates
- Key visuals

---

This structure mirrors professional label operations and scales naturally as the roster grows.
